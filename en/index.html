About me

My name is Oleg Avdeenko and I was born in 1989. I have graduated from Poltava University of Economics and Trade (Poltava, Ukraine) with a degree in Economic Cybernetics.
I started my career path in 2010 with projects at UCOZ - MobileMonkey (the site for mobile phones), sites for the cinema named after Kotlyarevskiy, the Listopad Leisure Palace, etc. Afterwards I studied CMS Drupal and due to it, I transferred all sites into it. In 2013, I was working as a system administrator as well as creating a website for the hotel "Kiev", the Recreation center in Zatoka. Then there were several projects on CMS Joomla which were created on my own. Since 2015 I developed CS GO roulettes on node.js and mongoDB, which included data parsing and browser emulation. Since 2016 I started developing plugins, templates for CMS DLE, Wordpress, Opencart and Drupal and configuring and installing them on a VPS server. My further diverse projects were written in pure PHP from scratch (Back-End and Front end, plus admin panels). Since 2018, I made 2 online stores based on the Laravel framework: a t-shirt store, a a lingerie store (96% completed). I have also written some extensions for Browser and Telegram bots.
